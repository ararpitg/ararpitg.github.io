Teaching

Philosophy paragraph below is synthesized from CV patterns, not a verbatim source document — rewrite freely.

My teaching bridges critical theory and hands-on practice — students in my courses don’t just read about platform capitalism, algorithmic bias, or ethnographic method, they build things: video essays, digital portfolios, fieldwork-based projects. I bring the same commitment to transparency and access that shapes my archival work into the classroom, particularly through inclusive grading design and curriculum that makes room for first-generation and underrepresented students to bring their own frameworks into contact with the material.
